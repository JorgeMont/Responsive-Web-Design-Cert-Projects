Blue Switch

The most popular options, this switch offers audio and tactile feedback when preseed.
The switch is actuated when pressing halfway down, this means that eventualy you can use this feedback to type faster by using less effort and not pushing the key all the way down.

  • Tactile feedback
  • Clicky sound when pressed

Red Switch

We could call this switch the opposite of the blue option, it doesn't provide a tactile feedback and the characteristic "clicky" sound is absent as well. That is why this option is the ideal to those who prefer a quiet response while typing.

  • Non tactile feedback
  • More quiet typing compared to the blue switch
  • Preferred by gamers

Brown Switch

The perfect option for those who like a tactile feedback but dont't like the "clicky" sound of the blue switches.

  • Tactile feedback
  • Silent when pressed
